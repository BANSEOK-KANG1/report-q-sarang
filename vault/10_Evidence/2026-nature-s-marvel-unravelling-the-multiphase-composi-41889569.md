## Abstract
This review article delves into the multiphase composition of <i>Cordyceps militaris</i> mushroom and explores its implications as a dietary supplement. <i>C. militaris</i> is a species of mushroom renowned for its medicinal properties and has a long history of use in traditional medicine. The multiphase composition of <i>C. militaris</i> encompasses bioactive compounds such as polysaccharides, cordycepin, adenosine, nucleosides, proteins, amino acids, sterols, and trace elements. These components may support immune health, energy, stamina, respiratory health, anti-inflammatory effects, antioxidant activity, and sexual health and fertility. However, more research is needed to understand C. militaris' mechanisms of action and confirm its efficacy in health applications. Safety, quality considerations, dosage recommendations, and potential interactions with other supplements or medications are also discussed. Overall, <i>C. militaris</i> mushroom holds significant promise as a multiphase dietary supplement, but careful evaluation and consultation with healthcare professionals are advised for optimal use.<h4>Supplementary information</h4>The online version contains supplementary material available at 10.1007/s13197-025-06531-5.
